Extracurricular Material

Python Refresher

Jupyter Notebooks, Numpy, and Pandas

Linear Algebra

Statistics

Please use this extracurricular content to refresh your knowledge on any of the above topics as necessary! The courses in the core curriculum are intended to prepare you for the projects in each module, but a little refresher never hurts.
